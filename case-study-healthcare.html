Centralized LMS for a Healthcare Academy

Case Study: Managing employee training and compliance for a regulated healthcare organization in the Middle East.

Industry Healthcare (KSA)
Role Project Manager
Focus Compliance & Regulated Learning

Table of Contents

  • Business Challenge
  • Solution & Approach

01. Business Problem / Challenge

The client, a KSA-based healthcare organization, was a first-time LMS adopter. They needed to manage training and track compliance in a highly regulated environment, but faced significant regional and organizational hurdles.

No Existing Framework

Evolving requirements due to the organization's first-time adoption of an LMS.

Regional Hosting

Cloud hosting limitations and data residency constraints within the KSA region.

Virtual Training

Critical dependence on live virtual training via third-party webinar platforms.

Operational Customization

Need for targeted UI and workflow customizations to support specific medical training needs.

02. Solution & Implementation Approach

We adopted an adaptive, phased implementation approach that prioritized regional compliance and user experience.

1

Regional Cloud Deployment

Deployed the LMS on a compliant alternative cloud environment to address KSA infrastructure and data residency constraints.

2

Coordinated API Workflows

Integrated the client's existing webinar platform using API workflows to enable automated attendance tracking for live sessions.

3

Tailored User Experience

Delivered specific customizations, including session color-coding and a bespoke registration experience, to align with healthcare operations.

4

Dual-Learner Ecosystem

Configured the system to support both internal employee compliance training and external professional education programs.

03. Results & Impact

The project transformed the organization's training culture and received high internal praise.

Centralized Tracking

Real-time visibility into training completion and compliance for all employees.

Seamless Delivery

Unified experience for live virtual training with automated attendance tracking.

High Adoption

Improved user adoption through workflows tailored to regional needs.

Business Expansion

Enabled the LMS academy to scale and support external learners.

Award Winning

Recognized internally as the 'Best Project' within the organization.

04. Key Learnings & Takeaways

  • Regional Awareness: Navigating local hosting and infrastructure is critical for success in the Middle East.
  • Change Management: First-time LMS adopters require strong guidance to define effective learning workflows.
  • Flexibility: An adaptive approach allows the platform to evolve into a multi-purpose training ecosystem.
  • API Power: Custom integrations can bridge the gap between legacy tools and modern LMS features.
